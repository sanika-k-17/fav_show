Tipendar Gada

Tipendra Jethalal Gada also known as Tapu, is a fictional character from the television series Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah. He is a college student and is the leader of "Tapu Sena". Tapu lives in the society with his mother Daya, father Jethalal and grandfather Champaklal Gada. The character was created by Asit Kumarr Modi and originally portrayed by Bhavya Gandhi Tapu's character is known as the smartest and naughtiest kid of the society. He is the leader of the "Tapu Sena", that include other children of the society (mainly Sonu, Goli, Gogi and Pinku).[5] His friendship with the Tapu Sena, and interaction with Bhide (played by Mandar Chandwadkar), is a source of entertainment and comic relieve.[6] Tapu has often given innovative ideas during several celebrations in the society and under difficult circumstances.[7



Gulabkumar Hansraj Hathi

Gulabkumar Hansraj "Goli" Hathi is son of Dr. Hansraj Hathi and Komal Hathi. He is an active member of Tapusena. He is strong and very cunning. Goli wants to enter into his father's footstep and aspires to become a doctor. In the initial years, he was also shown as a voracious eater but now Goli is a mature boy. He can't control when he sees food around and like to bust Jethalal.



Sonu Bhide

Sonalika Atmaram "Sonu" Bhide is the daughter of Atmaram Bhide and Madhavi Bhide. Sonu is also an active member of the Tapusena and is in the same college as that of Tapu, Goli and Pinku. She is Tapu's best friend and also the vice-captain of the group. She is a bright student, who has always scored good percentile and secured first position in the class. She's also very smart and judicious among whole group. She has often shown her culinary skills by making delicious foods. When Sonu and Tapu are too close to each other, Bhide often gets in the middle of them. She considers the whole of Tapusena as her brothers but she is the girlfriend of Tappu and might marry him in future episodes.



Pankaj Diwan Sahay

Pankaj Diwan "Pinku" Sahay is a member of Tapusena and once lived in the Gokuldham Society. Pinku studies one class higher of Tapu, Sonu and two classes higher than Goli. He is smart, intelligent and a brilliant student. Pinku is not dim-witted. It was found in Episode 2415 that his real parents, Diwan Sahay and Deepika Sahay, are undercover agents for the Government of India.



Gurucharan Singh Roshan Singh Sodhi

Gurucharan Singh "Gogi" Sodhi is the son of Roshan Singh Sodhi and Roshan Kaur Sodhi. He is the youngest member of the Gokuldham Society. He is similar to his father in nature. Gogi is also the member of Tapusena but is a class junior to them. He is the most pampered child of the society. He is often shown to be very naive as a kid. He is known to shout "Chak De Phatte" in joy.
